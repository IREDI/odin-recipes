Overnight Oats

Description

Delicous oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • choice of milk
  • strawberries and blue berries
  • honey or brown sugar

Directions

  1. In a container, mearue out 1/2 cup oats and 1/2 cups milk
  2. add in as many berries as you desire
  3. add 1-2 tablespoons of honey or 1-2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  4. put container in the fridge over night
